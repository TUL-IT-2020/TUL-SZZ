$C^k_n = \frac{}{}$
- $0 \leq k \leq n$


$C^k_n = 0$
- $0 \leq k \leq n$


$C^{-k}_n =C^{-k}_{-n}= 0$
- $0<k$
- $n \in N$

$C^k_{-n} = (-1)^k C^k_{n+k-1}$
- $0<n$
