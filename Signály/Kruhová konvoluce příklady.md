# Kruhová konvoluce

## Příklad 1
Mějme signál $x[n] = \{1, 2, 3, 4\}$ a $h[n] = \{1, 1, 1\}$.

### Postup
Délka výsledného signálu je: 
- $|x| + |h| - 1 = 4 + 3 - 1 = 6$

| $i$        | 0   | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   |
| ---------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| $x[0] * h$ | 1   | 1   | 1   | 0   | 0   | 0   |
| $x[1] * h$ | 0   | 2   | 2   | 2   | 0   | 0   |
| $x[2] * h$ | 0   | 0   | 3   | 3   | 3   | 0   |
| $x[3] * h$ | 0   | 0   | 0   | 4   | 4   | 4   |
| $sum()$    | 1   | 3   | 6   | 9   | 7   | 4   |

Konvoluce: $y[n] = \{1, 3, 6, 9, 7, 4\}$

| $x_1$ | 1   | 3   | 6   | 9   |
| ----- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| $x_2$ | 7   | 4   | 0   | 0   |
| $y =$ | 8   | 7   | 6   | 9   |

### Výsledek
Kruhová konvoluce: $y[n] = \{8, 7, 6, 9\}$

## Příklad
Kruhová konvoluce
$h = (1,2,3,4)$
$x = (2,0,-3,1)$

### Postup

| i | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|$h[1]*x$|2|0|-3|1|0|0|0|
|$h[2]*x$|0|4|0|-6|2|0|0| 
|$h[3]*x$|0|0|6|0|-9|3|0|
|$h[4]*x$|0|0|0|8|0|-12|4|
|sum()|2|4|3|3|-7|-9|4|

Konvoluce: $y = (2,4,3,3,-7,-9,4)$

Kruhová konvoluce:

| $x_1$ | 2   | 4   | 3   | 3   |
| ----- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| $x_2$ | -7  | -9  | 4   | 0   |
| $y =$ | -5  | -5  | 7   | 3   |
### Výsledek
Kruhová konvoluce: $y = (-5,-5,7,3)$
